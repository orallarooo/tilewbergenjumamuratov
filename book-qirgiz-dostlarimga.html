Qirgʻiz doʻstlarimga

Published in 2024

A poignant story of love, loss and the silence of the northern coast. This novel takes the reader to the vast expanses of the Aral Sea region, where the fate of the main character intertwines with the history of an entire generation.

Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the complex relationships between Karakalpak and Kyrgyz peoples, bound by common history and cultural ties. The narrative is woven with subtle observations about human nature, the passage of time, and the unbreakable bonds of friendship that transcend borders and political changes.
